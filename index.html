What is Kangen Water?

Kangen means "Return to Origin"—Electrolyzed Reduced Water (ERW) supercharged through electrolysis. This optimizes pH levels and activates molecular hydrogen antioxidants for enhanced health benefits.

The Science of Ionized Water

Kangen Water® is water produced by advanced water ionizer machines. For more than 5 decades, this technology has been used to help restore the body to its original, healthy and vibrant state.

Hydrogen Rich

High concentration of Molecular Hydrogen (H2) acts as an efficient antioxidant that diffuses rapidly across cell membranes.

Antioxidant

Helps reduce free radicals and oxidative stress, providing external antioxidant support for your daily diet.

Alkaline

Maintains optimal pH balance of 7.365, helping your body return to its natural alkaline state.

Negative ORP

Exhibits negative Oxidation-Reduction Potential, indicating higher antioxidizing potential for consumption.

The 7 Types of Kangen Water

Each type of water is specifically designed for different uses and health benefits.

pH 8.5-9.5

Kangen Water

Primary Use: Daily drinking water

Benefits: Helps maintain optimal pH balance, provides antioxidant support, promotes hydration

Description: The most popular choice for daily consumption. Rich in molecular hydrogen and antioxidants, this alkaline water helps restore your body's natural balance.

Usage Tips:

  • Drink 2-3 glasses upon waking
  • Consume 30 minutes before meals
  • Use for cooking rice and vegetables
pH 7.0

Clean Water

Primary Use: Baby formula preparation

Benefits: Neutral pH, pure and clean, safe for infants

Description: Purified water with neutral pH, ideal for preparing baby formula safely without interfering with nutritional content.

Usage Tips:

  • Prepare baby formula safely
  • Use for infant feeding
  • Ideal for sensitive applications
pH 6.0

Beauty Water

Primary Use: Skin and hair care

Benefits: Tightens pores, improves skin texture, enhances hair shine

Description: Mildly acidic water that acts as a natural astringent, perfect for skincare routines and hair care.

Usage Tips:

  • Use as facial toner
  • Final hair rinse after shampooing
  • Apply to skin after cleansing
pH 2.5

Strong Acidic Water

Primary Use: Sanitizing and disinfecting

Benefits: Natural disinfectant, kills bacteria, sanitizes surfaces

Description: Powerful acidic water with strong disinfecting properties, perfect for cleaning and sanitizing without harsh chemicals.

  • Disinfect fruits and vegetables
